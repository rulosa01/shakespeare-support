# Privacy Policy

**The Complete Shakespeare** collects no personal data.

The app is a self-contained reader for Shakespeare's plays, sonnets, and poems. Everything runs on your device.

- No accounts, sign-ins, or registration.
- No analytics, telemetry, or crash reporting.
- No advertising or third-party trackers.
- No data is sent to any external server.

## Apple Intelligence

The "Explain" feature uses Apple's on-device Foundation Models framework. The text you select and surrounding scene context are processed entirely by the local Apple Intelligence model running on your iPhone or iPad — they are never transmitted off the device, including to me as the developer.

## Children

The app contains the unabridged works of William Shakespeare and is rated 13+ on the App Store. It collects no information from anyone, including children under 13.
